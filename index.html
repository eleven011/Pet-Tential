Pet-Tential

Welcome to Pet-Tential! This app was created in an effort to encourage animal adoption. We've streamlined the online search process for you. Look through available shelter animals near you; customize their distance from you, animal type, fur color, and so much more. We hope you find your forever friend!

If you sign in with google, you are able to save your liked pets. Please note that if you choose to browse as a guest this feature has been turned off, but feel free to scroll through the pets!
